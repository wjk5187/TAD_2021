
What research question do you want to answer?

- Did Donald Trump’s candidacy, and eventual presidency, mark a shift in the Overton window?


What are two possible answers?

- Trump’s candidacy precipitated an observable shift in issues emphasized and rhetoric used in the context of certain issues (immigration, foreign intervention, trade, etc.) post-2015

- OR Trump’s candidacy did not bring any major shifts in the rhetoric used compared to pre-2015 political discourse. 


What are the documents of text data you will analyze?

- GOP convention speeches; ideally as far back as 1960s are earlier 
- RNC platforms 

Give two examples of documents in the corpus that you expect will be high in similarity to each other? 

- documents from 2016 and 2020 cycles // documents that are relatively close to 2016 cycle

Two examples that are *low* in similarity?

- documents from recent cycles and those from more distant past (ie 2016 vs 1988)
